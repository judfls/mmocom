About Jude Felstead



Jude Felstead aka Motor Machine Orchestra is a Canadian musician, coder, editor, and graphic designer. He moved to Los Angeles, California in 2017 and he still lives there now. He goes back to his hometown of Bramton, Ontario in the summer to see his family. He started making music at the age of eight, posting his music to his bandcamp. He makes music on Ableton Live 12 with his Novation Launchpad MK3 Mini. He took graphic design class in 2018 for 2 years and self-taught in coding. He knows HTML, CSS, and JS. He has a personal website at judefelstead.com, that has information about him, games, and interactive stories.
